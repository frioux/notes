The Tallest Man on Earth - Burden of Tomorrow

Tabbed by: Pontus
E-mail: ponza97@telia.com

Since there's no good tab on this wonderful song here on UG I thought I'd share a tab
that's probably 99% correct. If you have any thoughts on changes to be made on this tab be
sure to send an email. This is my first tab so be kind! :)

Tuning: CGCFCe

Second string is tuned up a half step,the rest (except the first string) is tuned down.

Capo on 6th fret.

I'll give you the chords for the different parts of the song. Just listen to the song to
get the chord changes right and to figure out the strumming pattern. It's pretty simple
and not hard to learn! :)
