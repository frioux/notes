The Mountain Goats - Tallahassee

This song is very easy to play, but Darnielle's awesome chord voicings make
it beautifully satisfying. Most of the credit for this goes to TMG Wiki, but
I fixed a few errors by watching John play it live here:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ICCWW40Vhog
There's a repeating bass riff (I think? There's definitely a bass playing this on
the 5th & 7th frets, but sound like it's in the guitar octave) tabbed below, and
John usually does some sort of embelishment on the end of each verse, so I put
the most common one below as well.

[Chords]
G/D:  x5540x or x55403 (John uses the first)
G:    320033
A5/G: xxx055
G5:   xxx033
A/E:  x776xx
All others play according to UG pop-ups :)
