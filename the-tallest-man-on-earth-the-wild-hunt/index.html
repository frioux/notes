The Tallest Man on Earth - The Wild Hunt

*Notes* - These chords won't sound exactly the same, as the song is
supposed to be in CFCFCF tuning. I don't fancy tuning to that anyways, so
this is for anyone else who wants to try and play it in standard.

- The intro (and where it is repeated) sounds fine without the Em* chords,
but I put them in anyway, just listen to the song to try and get how that
should sound.

- In the chorus ('...the wild hunt a comin'...') there is a run down of
notes. For this part I just let the top half of the G chord ring out, and
pick the bass notes individually. It looks like this:

e|--3-r-i-n-g---------------3------------|
B|--3-r-i-n-g---------------3------------|
G|--0-r-i-n-g---------------0------------|
D|--0-----------------------0------------|
A|--2--------3---2---0------2------------|
E|--3--------------------3---------------|

- The timing for the outro goes very loose so I haven't included bar lines -
  just listen to the song for the timing.
