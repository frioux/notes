2020-05-09

I was intending to make Chef John's above, but forgot to hydrate the beans ahead of time. My friend Michael linked to the Pressure Cooker version above and I had heard about this version of Jambalaya from C-4, which puts the rice in during the cook. C-4 said it was really good so I figured I'd do both at the same time.

I used the full Chef John's recipe, but did it in the Instant Pot. It was under pressure for 60m and should have been at least 75m. On top of that the rice during the cook turned to a gummy mush. I intend to try this again but without the rice in the cook. The sausage was amazing and the ham hock melted off the bone and was pristine.
