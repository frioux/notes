Monkey Bread

  • 4 cans refrigerator biscuits
  • 3/4 c sugar
  • 1 T cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 sticks butter
  • 1 T cinnamon (not a typo)
  • 1 c sugar
  • raisis, nuts, etc

Preheat oven to 350â„‰. Cut biscuits into quarters; place in a plastic back with 3/4 c sugar and 1 T cinnamon. Shake and then place pieces in Bundt pan or tube pan. DO NOT GREASE PAN! Dump leftover sugar and cinnamon from plastic bag in a saucepan; add butter, 1 T cinnamon and 1 c sugar; bring mixture to a boil. Pour over pieces in pan. Bake for approximately 35-40 minutes. Let cool for 5 minutes. Flip over on a plate. You can add raisins or nuts if you like.
