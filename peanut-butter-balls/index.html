Peanut Butter Balls

(read to me over the phone by Grandma)

  • 2c peanut butter
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 lb powdered sugar
  • 3c rice crispies

Mix till ingredients well covered. Refrigerate.

  • 8oz Hershey bar + 16 oz Chocolate Chips (24oz chips works too)
  • half bar parafin wax helps chocolate set

Use any leftover chocolate to make nut clusters

Addendum:

Melt the wax first in your double boiler before the chocolate. Once the water is boiling good, turn off the fire. Don't continue cooking.
