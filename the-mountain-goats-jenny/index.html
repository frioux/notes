The Mountain Goats - Jenny

Great song from one of John Darnielle's best albums. The strumming patterns are above,
but I think the best way to figure it out is playing along with the recording, especially
for the first half of the instrumental bit after "the pirate's life for me." I added
barlines in the chord chart for just that part of the song, so you can see visually
where he's creating a melody by moving the second note of the chord.
Here's a detailed tab of it:
